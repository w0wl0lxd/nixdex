- The trigram sidecar (`files.ngram.postings`) now stores native `roaring` bitmaps instead of raw `u32` ordinal lists (format v2, with v1 read compatibility). This run-length-encodes common trigrams to a fraction of the previous size and removes the per-query bitmap rebuild; candidate-ordinal intersection now loads and intersects postings lazily, smallest-posting-first, and short-circuits on an empty result.
